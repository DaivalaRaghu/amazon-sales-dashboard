# 🛒 Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 📊 Project Overview

The **Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and visualize Amazon product sales data.

This dashboard provides deep insights into **sales performance, product categories, regional sales distribution, and product-level analytics**.
It enables users to explore the dataset interactively and identify patterns that support **data-driven business decisions**.

The dashboard is designed with a **modern e-commerce analytics layout inspired by Amazon UI**, providing a user-friendly experience for exploring product and sales data.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

✔ Analyze overall sales performance
✔ Identify top-performing product categories
✔ Visualize sales trends across cities and states
✔ Provide detailed product-level analytics
✔ Enable interactive filtering for business insights

---

# 🧠 Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer important analytical questions such as:

🔎 Which states generate the highest Amazon sales?
📦 Which product categories sell the most?
📈 How do sales change over time?
🛍 Which individual products perform best?
⭐ How many reviews do products receive?

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

The Power BI dashboard consists of **four main interactive pages**:

---

## 🏠 1️⃣ Overview Page

The **Overview page** provides a high-level summary of overall sales performance.

### Key Metrics

📌 Total Sales
📌 Total Units Sold
📌 Total Sellers

### Visualizations

📊 Sales by State
📊 Sales by Category
📈 Sales Trend by City
📋 Order Status Filters

### Insights Provided

✔ Identify top-performing regions
✔ Understand category contribution to revenue
✔ Track sales growth trends

---

## 🛍 2️⃣ Products Page

The **Products page** provides a visual catalog of Amazon products.

### Features

🧾 Product category listing
🖼 Product image display
🔍 Interactive product selection
📊 Category navigation panel

### Insights

Users can explore different products and categories to analyze **individual product performance and availability**.

---

## 👗 3️⃣ Product View Page

The **Product View page** displays detailed analytics for a selected product.

### Metrics Displayed

💰 Sale Amount
📦 Units Sold
⭐ Number of Reviews

### Visualizations

📈 Units Sold Over Time
🗓 Date Range Filter
🖼 Product Image Display

### Insights

This page helps understand:

✔ Sales performance of individual products
✔ Customer engagement through reviews
✔ Product demand trends

---

## 🧾 4️⃣ Product Tooltip Page

The **Tooltip page** provides quick product insights when hovering over items.

### Tooltip Displays

📊 Sales Amount
📦 Units Sold
⭐ Reviews
📈 Mini Sales Trend Chart

### Purpose

This feature improves **user experience by providing quick analytics without navigating to another page**.
